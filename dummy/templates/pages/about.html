About Our Department Marks Portal

The Department Marks Portal is a dedicated academic management system designed to simplify and digitize the workflow for question paper creation, mark entry, and performance tracking within our department. It is built with separate access levels for Admin and Faculty, ensuring each role has tools tailored to their responsibilities.

Admin Role

Admins are the central coordinators of the portal with comprehensive management capabilities.

  • 1 Creating and editing question papers according to academic regulations
  • 2 Managing faculty accounts and setting up initial login credentials
  • 3 Overseeing assessment schedules and data standards compliance

Faculty Role

Faculty members enjoy streamlined access with their department email integration.

  • 1 Onboarded using official department email for secure access
  • 2 First-time login with secure password setup and database storage
  • 3 Access personalized dashboard with profile and class information
  • 4 Manage subjects with Mark Entry, Student Lists, and Exam Lists
  • 5 Upload marks via Excel templates or set answer keys for auto-evaluation
  • 6 System calculates Course Outcome attainment and CO1-CO6 percentages

Portal Benefits

By integrating all these functions into a single platform, the Department Marks Portal enhances academic efficiency, fosters transparency, and ensures faculty and administrators have quick access to the information they need.
